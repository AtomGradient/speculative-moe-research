### Key Findings
1. Cross-architecture SD has uniformly low acceptance rates (<4% for all models)
2. Despite low acceptance, SD provides meaningful speedup through batch verification
3. MoE speedup (1.18-1.22x) is moderate: better than Dense 4B (0.98x), less than Dense 9B (1.64x)
4. Larger γ increases speedup despite lower acceptance (batch verification amortizes overhead)
5. SD speedup on MoE decreases slightly with longer prompts (1.26x → 1.19x)
